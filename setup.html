Oura Display

Pair your Oura account with your Ray-Ban Display glasses using a Personal Access Token.

1Paste your Personal Access Token

Generate one at cloud.ouraring.com/personal-access-tokens. It looks like a long string of letters and numbers.

2Token verified Connected

Scan this QR with your phone's camera, or copy the URL below.

3Add to glasses

Open the Meta AI app on your phone → Devices → Display Glasses → App connections → Web apps → Add a web app. Paste the URL above.

Your token stays in your browser and the URL fragment only. Nothing is sent to any server other than Oura's API.
